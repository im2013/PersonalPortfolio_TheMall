C# Mockup

10-12-2024

Created the plant simulator first using C# within Visual Studio. This allowed me to have much more control of the script, rather than first using Unity Engine. If I had first used Unity, I think I would have gotten overwhelmed by the aspect of thinking about visuals when I wasn't ready.

I first decided to create all the variables (max plant height, preferred soil, etc.) and simulated the plant's growth agewise and the plant's growth stages as an adult plant. After this, I created map points to simulate planted plants within a console.
