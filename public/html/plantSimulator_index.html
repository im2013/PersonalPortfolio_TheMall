Plant Simulator Devlog

A plant simulator I plan to use for numerous more personal projects in future. I wanted an application that could emulate plant life, including germination and other plant stages, day/night growth and growing habits. This project aims to have 3D plants with several 'parts' such as leaves, flowers, etc. and a proper simulation of a map including weather, temperature changes, seasons (with a map simulating the different types of seasons around the world), altitude, soil types.
