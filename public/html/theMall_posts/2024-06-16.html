Prop Importation and Organisation

16-06-2024

Spent hours organising and re-sizing models as I had to convert from Blender files to .fbx files, and import into Unity.

At first, I created new numerous ice cream textures for a few hours, as I enjoy variety- this insigificant feature may be appreciated by few, but variety is the spice of life and ensures the player won't see repetitive objects over-and-over again.

After creating the icecream flavours and animations, (open lid, close lid, etc.) I began to import numerous models to Unity. I have not yet uploaded ALL of my completed models, and many, many models remain uncompleted as of now.
