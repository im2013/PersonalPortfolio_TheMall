6-08 onwards

After recovery, I decided to tackle the development of the phone. A very important aspect of gameplay used to expand the amount of interactions the player can have with their environment other than running, jumping and in future, hiding.

The player will have access to 8 apps:

  • Camera App

    Player can take photos of objects- where the subject of the object is stored and used for other apps.

  • Settings App

    Instead of a very unimmersive, and overused ESC menu- I decided I want the player to access settings through their phone instead.

  • Notes App

    Player can write notes here.

  • Message App

    Player can communicate to other friends apart of their Contacts. Used in game later during the Hide-And-Seek section.

  • Photos App

    Player can see recently taken photos here, and send them to other friends.

  • Contacts App

    Friends made will give you their contact, which will allow you to message them and send photos.

  • Map App

    Used as a map for the game, which is very useful as the mall can seem maze-like sometimes.

  • Doom App

    A joke suggested to me- I plan to have text appear saying "DOOM" and you scroll downwards- "DOOM SCROLLING".
