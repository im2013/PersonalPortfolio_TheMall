(18:55) Raycasting is the process of shooting a 'ray' from a point of origin, outwards in a specific direction.

I've used raycasting here for the player to be able to see what they are looking at. It works by shooting a ray out of the camera which then bounces forwards, hitting the collider of an object of a specific category, and returns the object at the ray's hit point.
